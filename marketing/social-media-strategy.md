# 30-Day Social Media Content Strategy

Complete social media content plan for BookkeepingPro Solutions across LinkedIn, Facebook, and Instagram.

---

## Platform Strategy Overview

### LinkedIn (Primary Platform)
**Target:** Business owners, decision-makers, B2B networking
**Post Frequency:** 3-5x per week
**Content Mix:** 
- 40% Educational tips
- 30% Case studies/results
- 20% Engagement posts (questions, polls)
- 10% Promotional

### Facebook
**Target:** Local business owners, community groups
**Post Frequency:** 3-4x per week
**Content Mix:**
- 35% Educational content
- 25% Behind-the-scenes
- 20% Client testimonials
- 20% Local community engagement

### Instagram
**Target:** Younger entrepreneurs, visual storytelling
**Post Frequency:** 4-5x per week (mix of feed + stories)
**Content Mix:**
- 40% Quick tips (graphics)
- 30% Stories/reels (short videos)
- 20% Behind-the-scenes
- 10% Motivational quotes

## Final Tips

1. **Be Consistent**: Post 3-5x/week minimum
2. **Provide Value First**: 80% education, 20% promotion
3. **Engage Authentically**: Actually respond to comments
4. **Track What Works**: Double down on high-performing content
5. **Stay On-Brand**: Professional but approachable
6. **Use Your Voice**: Be personal, not corporate
7. **Test & Iterate**: Try different formats and see what resonates

**Remember:** Social media is a long game. Consistency beats perfection. Show up, provide value, and the clients will come!
