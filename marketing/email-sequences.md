## Implementation Guide

### Email Platform Recommendations

**For Small Lists (<1,000 subscribers):**
- **Mailchimp** - Free up to 500 contacts
- **ConvertKit** - Free up to 300 subscribers
- **MailerLite** - Free up to 1,000 subscribers

**For Growing Lists:**
- **ActiveCampaign** - Best automation ($29/month)
- **HubSpot** - All-in-one CRM (Free tier available)

### Automation Setup Checklist

- [ ] Choose email platform
- [ ] Create account and import contacts
- [ ] Set up automation workflows
- [ ] Create email templates with your branding
- [ ] Set up tracking and analytics
- [ ] Test all sequences before going live
- [ ] Monitor open rates and click rates
- [ ] A/B test subject lines
- [ ] Optimize based on performance

### Key Metrics to Track

- **Open Rate:** Aim for 20-30%
- **Click Rate:** Aim for 3-5%
- **Conversion Rate:** Aim for 2-5% (lead to consultation)
- **Unsubscribe Rate:** Keep below 0.5%

---

## Customization Tips

1. **Personalize:** Always use [First Name] and reference their specific lead magnet
2. **Add Stories:** Replace generic examples with real client stories
3. **Update Numbers:** Use your actual stats and results
4. **Test:** A/B test subject lines and CTAs
5. **Segment:** Create different sequences for construction vs. real estate leads

---

**Remember:** The goal is to provide value, build trust, and create opportunities for conversation. Don't be overly salesy!
