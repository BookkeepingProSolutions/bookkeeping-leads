## Outreach Strategy Guide

### Best Practices

**Email Timing:**
- Send Tuesday-Thursday
- 8-10am or 1-3pm local time
- Avoid Mondays and Fridays

**Response Rates:**
- Cold email: 1-5% response rate
- Expect 1-3 consultations per 100 emails sent
- Close rate: 20-40% of consultations

**Weekly Outreach Goal:**
- 25-50 personalized emails per week
- 10-20 LinkedIn connection requests per week
- Follow up 3 times per prospect

### Personalization Checklist

Before sending ANY email:
- [ ] Research the company (website, recent projects, news)
- [ ] Find a specific detail to mention (project, award, growth)
- [ ] Customize the pain point to their situation
- [ ] Check for spelling/grammar errors
- [ ] Verify all links work
- [ ] Send test email to yourself first

### Tracking & Optimization

Use a simple spreadsheet to track:
- Date sent
- Company name
- Template used
- Response (Yes/No/Maybe)
- Consultation booked (Yes/No)
- Deal closed (Yes/No)

Optimize based on what's working!
